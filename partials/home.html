ShockLock is all about SAFETY

ShockLock is a tool that you can use when you need to hold up a hatch with worn-out GCS. The mechanical efficiency is maximized by taking the load off the compressed gas found in GCS.
One Handed Application

ShockLock is easy to use and you can install/uninstall it with only one hand.

Supports 100 lbs of Weight

ShockLock is strong! ShockLock supports 100 lbs of weight while the average hatch is 40lbs.

Non Damaging

ShockLock is made from 100% non-damaging aluminium.
